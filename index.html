Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Library Saas ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the Library Saas Android application.

When you use our application, we may collect the following information:

  • Full Name

We do not collect location data, photos, contacts, advertising identifiers, crash reports, or analytics data.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used for the following purposes:

3. Subscription Access

Library Saas provides a 30-day free trial. After the trial period, access may be granted upon successful payment confirmation. Payments are handled externally and are not processed within the app. We do not store payment information.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is securely stored on servers hosted by Render. We take reasonable technical and administrative measures to protect your information from unauthorized access or disclosure.

5. Push Notifications

We use Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) to send notifications related to your account or service updates. This requires storing a device-specific FCM token.
